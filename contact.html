Mailing list

The best way to talk about Digital-Scratch (installation, build, bugs, features, compatibility,...) is to send an email to the mailing list digitalscratch@googlegroups.com

To subscribe, send an email with whatever content to digitalscratch+subscribe@googlegroups.com and confirm the subscription.

History

Cyril Coquilleau and Julien Rosener founded Digital-Scratch in 2004 as a school project (UTBM).
